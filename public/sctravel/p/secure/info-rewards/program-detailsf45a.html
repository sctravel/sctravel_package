Hotel Price Guarantee - for Rewards members only!

We're so confident you'll find the best price for your hotel on Expedia we double guarantee it. Not only are Rewards Members eligible for the Best Price Guarantee that covers you for the first 24 hours after you book, you also receive our Hotel Price Guarantee that ensures you have the best hotel price up until midnight before you begin your stay.

Taking advantage of this benefit is easy

  • Book your hotel as a signed-in Expedia Rewards member.
  • Ensure the lower price you found is for identical stay dates.
  • Submit your claim prior to midnight of the check-in date at the hotel.
  • Make a claim for a hotel that is not part of a package.
  • Check that the site where you saw the lower price is in English and the price is in dollars.
  • Verify the hotel, room type, rate plan and cancellation policy are the same.

If everything matches, grab your itinerary and begin your claim.

Earn Elite Plus status

The more you travel, the sooner you'll unlock the special benefits reserved exclusively for Elite Plus members. You'll receive special offers and discounts on a regular basis as well as 25% bonus Expedia points every time you book an eligible hotel or vacation package.

  • Free Hotel Upgrades
    Free Room Upgrades at VIP Access™ Hotels

    Enjoy exclusive perks at over 1,300 VIP Access hotels worldwide. Elite Plus members receive free room upgrades and more upon availability at check-in.

    Elite Plus members also receive:
    • Flexible check-in and check-out
    • Other bonus perks (these vary by hotel)
    How you get it:
    • To find participating hotels, look for the VIP Access badge when booking on Expedia or search for VIP Access hotels.
    • Then, when checking in, show your Elite Plus card at the hotel's front desk to receive your free upgrade and more.
    Browse VIP Access Hotels
  • Priority Customer Care

    Receive express customer service from an Elite Plus travel specialist, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

    How you get it:
    • Call Elite Plus priority customer care at 1-800-397-1207 or send an email to eliteplus@expedia.com.
    • For international calls placed from a landline*:
      • Inform the local telephone operator that you want to make a collect call to the United States and give the operator this number: +1-702-939-2635.
      • When the call is placed, you will be connected to an Elite Plus travel specialist. (And, of course, there's no charge to you.)

    *Costs associated with calls placed on mobile phones will not be reimbursed.

    Always get the best hotel price — even if it's not ours. If you find a lower price online up until the night before you travel, we'll match it.*

    Earn 25% bonus Expedia Rewards Points on all hotels and packages that include a hotel.

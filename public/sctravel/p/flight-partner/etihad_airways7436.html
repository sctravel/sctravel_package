          Plan your next trip with Etihad Airways from Los Angeles International Airport!

          Daily non-stop service from Los Angeles to Abu Dhabi will commence on June 1, 2014. Etihad Airways will offer maximum connectivity, via Abu Dhabi, with 25 destinations across the Middle East region and Indian Subcontinent, 12 of which are in India, catering for the demand from Los Angeles' diverse and multicultural population. Los Angeles will be Etihad Airways' fourth U.S. destination, joining Chicago, New York and Washington DC, and will see the airline provide 35 return flights each week between the UAE and U.S. The new service will cater for growing leisure and business traffic demands in Los Angeles, as well as the considerable catchment area of the city.

          Los Angeles, one of the world's most iconic, popular, and multicultural destinations with a metropolitan population area of more than 18 million people, is the second largest in the US. It is classified as a 'global city' with strengths in business, international trade, entertainment, culture, media, fashion, science, sports, technology, education, medicine and research.

          Etihad Airways, five times voted World's Leading Airline*, will deploy a three cabin aircraft on the Los Angeles operation. Flight EY170, will depart Los Angeles at 4:50 p.m. and arrive in Abu Dhabi at 8:10 p.m. the following day. The return flight, EY171 will depart daily from Abu Dhabi at 8:50 a.m. and arrive in Los Angeles at 2:20 p.m. the same day.

At Etihad, we're all about making the journey with us comfortable and hassle-free both on the ground and in the air.

          US Customs and Border Protection (CBP) facility at Abu Dhabi Airport:

          The United States Customs and Border Protection (CBP) facility at Terminal 3 in Abu Dhabi Airport is a purpose-built facility that allows U.S. bound passengers to undertake all immigration, customs and agriculture inspections in Abu Dhabi prior to departure.

          Abu Dhabi Airport is one of only a handful of airports outside North America that offer the CBP facility. With our U.S immigration pre-clearance facility, guests will now be able to pre-clear the U.S. Customs and Border Protection formalities before boarding their flights in Abu Dhabi. Upon arrival in the U.S., guests will be treated as though they are domestic arrivals. Shorter connection times are possible for onward flights in the U.S., with your luggage arriving to the final destination.

          Etihad Airways operates flights to New York (EY101 only), Chicago (EY151) Washington (EY131) and Los Angeles* (EY171). Guests flying to these destinations in the U.S. will use the CBP facility at Abu Dhabi Airport.

          This service is not available for the Etihad Airways flight number (EY103) to New York (JFK).

          * Los Angeles flights from June 1, 2014

          Dubai, it's quicker via Abu Dhabi Travelling to Dubai has never been easier.

          Why not try flying into Abu Dhabi, before taking advantage of Etihad's free luxury coach service into the heart of Dubai — you'll be poolside quicker, having avoided the traffic hotspots and expensive airport transfer costs.




          Etihad have made it easier and faster for those travelling into Abu Dhabi to get to Dubai more conveniently. Free luxury Etihad Express coaches run throughout the day and night to two locations in the heart of Dubai from Abu Dhabi Airport, our hub, and back in the other direction.

          Etihad Diamond First Class and Pearl Business Class guests can either use our luxury Etihad Express coaches or book a free chauffeur service to and from anywhere in the UAE and at 24 other destinations on our network.

          Express Coach - Marina Mall stop (Airport Code: XNB)
          Dubai Marina Mall is located right on the outskirts of Dubai heading in the direction of Abu Dhabi. It is alongside Sheikh Zayed Road, between Dubai Marina and Jumeirah Lake Towers metro stations.

          It takes about 60 minutes to travel between Marina Mall and Abu Dhabi Airport.

          This bus stop is ideal for passengers originating from or heading to Umm Suqeim, Al Barsha, Dubai Marina, The Greens, Emirates Hills, Jumeirah Beach Residence, Jumeirah Lake Towers, The Gardens, the Green Community and Arabian Ranches.

          Express Coach service - Chelsea Towers stop
          (Airport Code: XMB)
          Chelsea Towers is located in the centre of Dubai, close to the city's major shopping destinations, tourist spots, beaches and leisure parks. It is between Shangri-La and Towers Rotana hotels on Sheikh Zayed Road and is right next to the Financial Centre metro station. It is also not far from Dubai Mall and Burj Khalifah, the world's tallest building.
          It takes about 75 minutes to travel between Chelsea Towers and Abu Dhabi Airport.

          Dubai city check-in facility
          To make your journey even more comfortable, we offer you the option to check in at Chelsea Towers on Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai.

          • Check in is free at Chelsea Towers any time from 6-24 hours before your flight.
          • Check in your luggage and recieve your boarding card.
          • Hop aboard one of our free Etihad Express coaches direct to Abu Dhabi International Airport.

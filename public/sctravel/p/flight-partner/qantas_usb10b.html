        Comfort and convenience in International Economy


        Your Seat

        • Sit back and relax in our ergonomically designed, slimline seats with built-in lumbar support for your comfort.
        • Adjustable headrest and seat recline are sure to keep you comfortable for the duration of your journey.
        • Softer cushioning, a netted footrest and more legroom for additional comfort on longer journeys.
        • Designed by Marc Newson and built by Recaro.

        Onboard Dining

        • Refreshed Economy meal service is customised by flight duration and time of day, with more flexibility and choice.
        • On selected international flights, you'll be treated to a continuous refreshment service every 60-90 minutes and, to add variety, a third main meal option for long haul flights.
        • On the A380s and refurbished B747s you'll find a self-serve snack bar.

        Entertainment

        • Sit back and watch our personal on-demand, in-flight entertainment system throughout your journey.
        • Access 1500 options on our A380 and refurbished B747 aircraft using your in-seat control or touch screen monitor.
        • Options include a variety of movies, TV programs, CD playlists, moving maps, interactive games and radio channels.

        Amenities

        • On selected flights you'll receive a complimentary comfort kit to help you stay refreshed.
        • Your handy zip lock pouch contains eyeshades, toothbrush and toothpaste. Socks are available on request.
        • If you're travelling on one of our long haul flights you'll be provided with a soft blanket and cotton pillow to keep you cosy throughout your flight.

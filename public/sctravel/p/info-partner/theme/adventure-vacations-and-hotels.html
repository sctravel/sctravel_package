              Discover & Book Adventure Travel & Vacations

              With Expedia, you can find the adventure vacation you've been searching for. If you're looking for eco-oriented activities, consider a Vancouver vacation. In this beautiful coastal city renowned for its "greenness", you'll be close to thrilling heli-skiing at Whistler and 500,000 acres of scenic wilderness at nearby Garibaldi Provincial Park. If you prefer a warmer climate, check out Hawaii vacations for scuba diving, volcano hikes and surfing on some of the world's most awe-inspiring beaches. If its history and culture you crave, you can go a Machu Picchu vacation in Peru to learn about the fascinating ancient Inca. Whatever type of adventure you're looking for, Expedia can help you find the thrill of a lifetimeall you have to do is go for it!

              Find & Compare Adventure Hotels

              On your adventure vacation, you'll need accommodations. Expedia can help you find everything from budget hotels to ultra-luxe resorts all over the world. If you're going on an adventure in South Africa, Expedia can connect you with deals on Cape Town hotels. After a long day of white water rafting, off-roading or mountain biking in Maob, Utah you can choose from a number of lodging options with Expedia's deals on Moab hotels. Embarking on expedition through Alaska? Check out hotels in Anchorage for some places to stay in the Frontier State. No matter what kind of adventure you're on, Expedia will guide you to the accommodations best-suited for your trip.

Applying is fast and easy

Applying for the card is easy and it takes just a few minutes.

  1. Choose the APPLY button for the card you prefer and complete the application process.

  2. If approved, you'll be provided with a card number that can be used for booking travel immediately.
  3. Book a trip on Expedia with your brand-new card and automatically receive up to $100 as a statement credit.

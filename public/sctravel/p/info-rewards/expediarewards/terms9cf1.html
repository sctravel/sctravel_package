The terms "you" and "your" throughout these Terms and Conditions refer to any Member and any customer eligible to participate in the Program.

The Program has no predetermined termination date and may continue until such time as Expedia decides to terminate the Program. Expedia

may terminate the Program at any time. Upon termination, you will have ninety (90) days from the date Program termination is announced to use available Expedia Rewards Points remaining in your account. After that date, any Points will be forfeited without compensation.

Your continued participation in the Program constitutes your acceptance of any changes to these Terms and Conditions. You are responsible for remaining knowledgeable as to any changes that Expedia may make to these Terms and Conditions. The most current version of these Terms and Conditions will be available on the website at www.expedia.com/rewards and will supersede all previous versions of these Terms and Conditions.

Expedia makes no guarantees, warranties, or representations of any kind concerning the Program. Expedia Rewards Point earning and rewards redemptions are void where prohibited by applicable federal, state, or local laws or regulations and are subject to change as may be necessary to comply with such laws or regulations. You release Expedia and its affiliates and partners from all liability regarding the earning, redemption, and use of Expedia Rewards Points, including any rewards that, after receipt, may be lost, stolen, or destroyed. Expedia suppliers are in no way affiliated with or responsible for Expedia Rewards Program administration.

  • Enrollment

    You must have an account on Expedia.com in order to enroll in Expedia Rewards. Upon enrollment, you will be issued a Rewards ID number that is linked to your Expedia account ("Rewards Account"). To earn Expedia Rewards Points for a booking on Expedia, you must have a valid Rewards Account and be signed in to your Rewards Account at time of booking. No Points will be earned for bookings made on Expedia.com when you are not signed in to your Rewards Account.

    You must be an enrolled Member of Expedia Rewards prior to making a booking in order to earn Points for any eligible travel. If you enroll in Expedia Rewards after booking but before travel is completed, Points will not accrue on that booking. As an enrolled Member of the Program, you will receive regular account servicing updates by email. Members may opt out of participation in the Program from the Rewards Summary page within My Account on Expedia.com. Your ability to earn and redeem Expedia Rewards Points will be suspended after you opt out. If you opt out of Program participation, you will no longer have access to your account statement information, no Expedia Rewards Points will accumulate on future bookings, and any accumulated Expedia Rewards Points will be unavailable for redemption. Any Points in your Rewards Account at the time of opting out will expire 14 days from your opt-out date.

  • Earning Points

    You must be signed in to your Rewards Account prior to completing an eligible booking in order to earn Points for that booking. Points will be awarded only to the owner of the Rewards Account through which any eligible booking is made. Additional individuals named as travelers on the itinerary will not earn Points. If booking a trip for another individual as a travel arranger using the Arranger Settings in your Expedia account, the individual's account you are using to book the travel will earn Points for the eligible booking only if their Expedia account is enrolled in the Program and they have an active Rewards Account.

    After making an eligible booking, the anticipated amount of Expedia Rewards Points to be awarded will be held in pending status until thirty (30) days after travel is complete. Pending Expedia Rewards Points are not available for redemption usage. Thirty (30) days after completion of travel, Points will move from pending to available status. Once in available status, Expedia Rewards Points are eligible to be used by the Member for redemption purposes. The amount of pending Expedia Rewards Points calculated at the time of booking is based on the details of the itinerary booked. Changes or cancellations to the booked itinerary may result in differences between the estimated amount of pending Points calculated and the ultimate amount of Points that post to the Member's Rewards Account for that itinerary. No Expedia Rewards Points will be earned for change or cancellation fees imposed by suppliers.

    Expedia reserves the right to rescind at any time any pending or available Expedia Rewards Points that were earned or awarded for unused travel items. Expedia Rewards Points will be rescinded in cases where a Member receives a refund, from either Expedia or a travel supplier or vendor.

    Expedia has the right to monitor all Member Rewards Account activity. If your Rewards Account shows signs of fraud, abuse, or suspicious activity, Expedia may close or freeze your Rewards Account immediately, and you may lose your accrued Expedia Rewards Points. If you have conducted any fraudulent activity, Expedia reserves the right to take any necessary legal action and may have grounds to confiscate any rewards redeemed as a result of such activity. In addition, you may be liable for monetary losses to Expedia, including litigation costs and damages, and you will not be allowed to participate in Expedia Rewards in the future. Bookings discovered to be fraudulent will have their Expedia Rewards Points rescinded, and the Rewards Account associated with such fraudulent bookings will be frozen from further Rewards Point earning or redemption activity. To contest freezing of an account or the rescinding of Expedia Rewards Points, please call 1-800-EXPEDIA (1-800-397-3342).

    If your Rewards Account does not represent the correct number of Expedia Rewards Points that you should have been awarded as determined by Expedia, Expedia reserves the right to adjust your Point balance. If you believe your Rewards Account has been the subject of any suspicious activity, please contact the Expedia Service Center immediately. If it is determined you have been the victim of a fraud, the Points you have earned will be transferred to a new Rewards Account.

    Rewards Point accumulation and redemption activity can be accessed on your Rewards Activity page. Expedia Rewards Points and rewards have no cash value. Expedia Rewards Points may not be assigned, sold, transferred, and/or pledged to any third party. You have no property rights or other legal interests in Expedia Rewards Points. You are responsible for any personal tax liability that may be related to participation in Expedia Rewards and redemption of any rewards.

    If a Member believes he or she did not receive the correct amount of Expedia Rewards Points for a booking, the Member must contact Expedia Customer Service at 1-800-EXPEDIA (1-800-397-3342) within 90 days from the travel end date. Expedia will make the final determination as to whether Points adjustments of any kind are justified for the travel in question. In no event will any Expedia Rewards Points manually be moved from pending to available status sooner than thirty (30) days after completion of travel.

  • Travel Items Eligible for Earning Expedia Points

    Earn one Expedia Rewards Point for every five U.S. dollars actually spent on the following eligible travel items booked through Expedia.com when signed in to your Rewards Account:

    • Flights

    Earn one Expedia Rewards Point for every U.S. dollar actually spent on the following eligible travel items booked through Expedia.com when signed in to your Rewards Account:

    • Flight + Car booked via vacation packages tab

    Earn two Expedia Rewards Points for every U.S. dollar actually spent on the following eligible travel items booked through Expedia.com when signed in to your Rewards Account:

    • Hotels
    • Activities and services
    • Hotel + Air vacation packages
    • Hotel + Car vacation packages
    • Hotel + Air + Car vacation packages
    • Hotel + Air + Activities vacation packages
    • Hotel + Car + Activities vacation packages
    • Hotel + Air + Car + Activities vacation packages
  • Exclusions

    The following travel items are not eligible to earn Expedia Points:

    • Stand-alone rental cars
    • Vacation rentals via HomeAway
    • Cruises
    • Insurance
  • Bonus Point Offers

    From time to time, Expedia may offer opportunities to earn bonus Expedia Rewards Points. All applicable terms and conditions associated with the specific bonus offer must be met in order to earn bonus Expedia Rewards Points for your booked and completed travel. Bonus Expedia Rewards Points will be posted to your Rewards Account as pending at time of booking, and will move to available status thirty (30) days after the travel end date, unless otherwise specified in the promotion-specific terms and conditions. Bonus Expedia Rewards Point offers apply to changed bookings as long as the changed booking still meets all requirements and conditions of the offer. Bonus Point offers do not apply to cancelled bookings. Offers are limited to the offer recipient and are not transferable. Bonus Rewards Point offers are not redeemable for cash. Void where prohibited by law.

  • Points Expiration

    Expedia Rewards Points will not expire as long as there is a qualifying purchase or redemption activity through Expedia.com on your Rewards Account at least once every 18 months. "Qualifying purchase or redemption activity" means a Rewards-eligible trip or travel item which has been both booked and completed or redemption of a travel reward through your Rewards Account. All Points in your Rewards Account will expire if there is no qualifying purchase or redemption activity for a period of 18 months. Once expired, Expedia Rewards Points will not be reinstated.

  • Point Redemption

    Rewards include airline tickets and hotel coupons valid on bookings at Expedia Rate properties. Rewards cannot be redeemed for cash and have no cash value or residual cash value. Rewards cannot be combined with any other certificates, coupons, discounts, upgrades, awards, or promotions. Rewards are offered as promotional items and are void if sold for cash or other consideration, or if altered or copied, and cannot be reissued if destroyed, lost or stolen. Rewards are not valid where prohibited by law.

    In order to qualify for a reward, a Member must have a sufficient amount of available Expedia Rewards Points in his or her Rewards Account to redeem for the full cost of the reward in points. Pending Expedia Rewards Points cannot be used toward reward redemption. All redemptions are final and may not be cancelled.

Expedia, Inc. has the final authority on all decisions regarding reward ticketing, pricing, and availability and the interpretation of these Expedia Rewards Terms and Conditions. The point cost of Expedia Rewards flight rewards are based on variable amounts. These amounts are based on a combination of factors including itinerary, date and time of travel, and dollar value of travel. The amount of Expedia Rewards Points needed to redeem flight rewards can be searched on www.expedia-rewards.com. The following policies and terms apply to flight rewards:

    • Taxes, fees, and surcharges for the booking are included in the number of Expedia Rewards Points required for redemption and are subject to change without notice.
    • Flights may be nonstop, direct, or involve a change of planes. They may also be one-way flights.
    • Flights do not have to be exact roundtrips. Flights into and out of different airports are permitted.
    • Airline participation may vary from market to market.
    • Holds cannot be placed on Expedia flight reward reservations.

    For information regarding airline liability limitations, baggage liability, and other regulations of the Warsaw Convention and other regulations, please consult your air carrier.

    Only the Rewards Account holder can redeem Expedia Rewards Points, but the reward flight may be booked either in the name of the Member or in the name of any other person the Member designates. Once tickets are issued, flight rewards are not transferable and may be used only by the individual named on the ticket.

    Redeemed Expedia Rewards Points will be deducted from your Rewards Account at the time of booking. No Rewards Point amounts, reward availability, or dates of travel are confirmed until the required number of Expedia Rewards Points has been deducted from the Member's Rewards Account and the tickets or travel documents have been issued.

    All airline tickets booked in exchange for Expedia Rewards Points are nonrefundable, non-endorsable, and nontransferable, and cannot be changed for any reason. Travelers' names cannot be changed after booking. There are no cancellations of any flight reward reservation once it has been booked.

    Airline schedules, terms, points of service, and fares are subject to change without notice. Airline schedule changes, flight cancellations, interruptions, and oversold flights are outside of the control of Expedia. These situations must be remedied by the applicable travel supplier and may be subject to Federal or State Regulations. If an airline declares bankruptcy, it is not obligated to carry you or to refund tickets issued before the bankruptcy. Expedia is not allowed to refund tickets on airlines that have declared bankruptcy. Expedia Rewards Points redeemed or money given to Expedia immediately becomes the property of the airline and Expedia is required by law to comply with the airline's orders.

    Airline tickets will be issued as e-tickets unless paper tickets are required by the airline. For bookings made online at www.expedia-rewards.com, electronic confirmation and documentation will be sent to the e-mail address displayed on the Billing and Delivery page. The Member is responsible for destroyed, lost, and/or stolen rewards, and any fees imposed to replace such travel documents.

  • Hotel Redemptions

    Rewards Account holders can redeem available Expedia Rewards Points for Expedia coupons valid at Expedia Rate hotels in various increments ("Reward Coupon"). To redeem your available Expedia Rewards Points for a Reward Coupon, click here. Upon redeeming Expedia Rewards Points for a Reward Coupon in your selected amount, a Reward Coupon code valid for a minimum period of 12 months for one-time use only will be issued to the Member. The issued Reward Coupon will be deposited into the Member's Expedia Account, and can be found on the Coupons page within "My Account." Once the Reward Coupon code is issued, the Reward Coupon may be redeemed by the Member. The expiration date for each Rewards Coupon will be confirmed at the time of issuance. Redeemed Expedia Rewards Points will be deducted from your Rewards Account at the time the Reward Coupon code is issued. No Rewards Point amounts or reward availability is confirmed until the required number of Expedia Rewards Points has been deducted from the Member's Rewards Account and the Reward Coupon is issued.

    Reward Coupons can only be redeemed for bookings at Expedia Rate hotels on Expedia.com. After you select your eligible hotel booking, use the "I have a coupon" link on the booking summary page, and apply the Reward Coupon to your booking. A Reward Coupon can only be applied to one (1) hotel room per booking. A Reward Coupon cannot be used for booking multiple rooms on the same itinerary. Reward Coupons have no cash or residual cash value; if the full value of the coupon is not used, the remaining balance will be lost. Reward Coupons may not be combined with or applied to any other certificates, upgrades, awards, or coupons. All taxes, fees, charges, and surcharges applicable to the booking, including, without limitation, extra person, parking, phone, and other charges that are applicable to the reservation are the responsibility of the traveler, and Reward Coupons cannot be applied to such amounts. These charges must be paid at the time the reservation is confirmed or to the hotel directly, and they are the responsibility solely of the traveler. Reward Coupons cannot be applied to the cost of cancellation or change penalties, prepaid reservations, administrative fees, or other miscellaneous charges. Reward Coupon cash value 1/100¢. Void where prohibited by law.

    No Expedia Point refunds will be issued for unused or expired Rewards Coupons.

  • Hotel Price Guarantee

    The Hotel Price Guarantee is available to Expedia Rewards members in good standing. In the unlikely event that you find a lower rate for a hotel you have booked on www.expedia.com or through the Expedia call center at 1-800-EXPEDIA, whether on Expedia or on another U.S.-based website, we will honor the lower price. Hotels booked as part of a package are not eligible for the Hotel Price Guarantee. The guarantee does not apply to non-refundable rate plans and you must be signed in at the time of booking. The guarantee is subject to these terms and conditions.

    Notifying Expedia of a Claim:

    You may make a claim under the Hotel Price Guarantee from the time you book your standalone hotel on Expedia until 11:59 p.m. local time at the hotel's location, the day prior to hotel check-in. To make a claim, please complete this form, select your qualifying itinerary, and provide details on the lower price found, including screen shots. The lower price must be available for booking at the time you contact us, as determined by Expedia Rewards.

    Must be "Apples to Apples" Comparison:

    The Hotel Price Guarantee is available only for exact itinerary matches, including property, room type, rate plan, applicable refund policy, and the exact same dates of travel as booked through Expedia. The comparison must be to the same product purchased standalone through another website (i.e., not part of a hotel + air travel package).

    The Hotel Price Guarantee applies to the cost of booked travel, as presented in the Expedia search results, plus any taxes and fees. The Hotel Price Guarantee is not available for bookings on websites where the property, itinerary, or similar booking details are unknown until after purchase. You must meet all requirements imposed on the lower price (if any), including, without limitation, residency, regional, and age-related requirements.

    There is no limit on the number of Hotel Price Guarantee claims you may make per booking. However, any credit subsequent to the initial credit will be based on the amount of the previously credited price, not on the amount of the original price that you booked.

    Comparison Rates Must Be Available to the General Public Online:

    The Hotel Price Guarantee applies only to prices both advertised and available to the general public on a U.S.-based website. For example, this does not include rates offered on membership program websites; corporate discounts or rates; group, charter, rewards program, incentive, meeting, convention, consolidator, or interline prices; prices obtained via auction or similar process; or prices available only by using a coupon or other promotion not offered to the general public. The lower rate may not come from a website where you call to get the rate or from an e-mail that you received. The rate must be quoted and booked in U.S. dollars (and without reference to currency converters).

    Verification of Claims:

    All requests are subject to verification by Expedia. Expedia will not accept screenshots or other purported evidence of a lower price that cannot be independently confirmed by Expedia personnel. Nor will Expedia verify any request that it believes, in its sole discretion, is the result of a printing or other error or is made fraudulently or in bad faith. For verification of non-prepaid hotel bookings, the customer service representative will instruct you to mail a copy of your hotel receipt to Expedia within thirty (30) days after you have completed the hotel stay. Expedia will honor the lower price for verified requests. This may happen in one of several ways.

    In some cases, it will take the form of a credit to the credit card used for booking. In other cases, it may be necessary for the travel specialist to cancel your booking and re-book your stay at the lower price. You will not be charged any change or cancellation fees in connection with the Hotel Price Guarantee. In all cases, the travel specialist will explain the process to you.

    Changes:

    Expedia reserves the right in its sole discretion to modify or discontinue the Hotel Price Guarantee or to restrict its availability to any person, at any time, for any or no reason, and without prior notice or liability to you. The terms that are in effect at the time of your claim will determine your eligibility under the Hotel Price Guarantee. The failure by Expedia to enforce any provision of these terms and conditions shall not constitute a waiver of that provision.

  • Elite Plus® Status Qualification

    Expedia® Elite Plus® status is available to Expedia Rewards members who book and complete at least U.S. $10,000 worth of qualifying travel or book and stay at least fifteen (15) hotel nights within a calendar year. Qualifying travel for Elite Plus status includes flights, hotels, car rentals, activities, services and packages. It does not include cruises or vacation rentals via HomeAway. Only bookings made in your enrolled Expedia Rewards account qualify toward Elite Plus status. All dollar amounts are calculated after Expedia fees have been applied but before third-party taxes and fees. The Expedia Rewards member making the bookings will be given credit for all bookings made to that account, regardless of who completes the travel. No credit will be given for cancelled bookings or for bookings made via single-use accounts. Two or more individuals may not combine separate accounts.

    Certain Elite Plus offers and benefits may not be available to members who are not U.S. residents.

    Once upgraded to Elite Plus, your status is valid for the remainder of the calendar year in which you qualified, the next full calendar year, and through February 28 of the following year, unless otherwise specified.

    Elite Plus status and its benefits are not transferable and are not redeemable for cash. Expedia reserves the right in its sole discretion to discontinue or modify Elite Plus status, or to restrict the program's availability to any person, at any time, for any or no reason, and without prior notice or liability to you. Expedia's failure to enforce any provision of these Terms & Conditions shall not constitute a waiver of that or any other provision. Expedia's interpretation of these Terms & Conditions is final. These Terms & Conditions may not be changed by any unauthorized person, including employees of Expedia, Inc.
